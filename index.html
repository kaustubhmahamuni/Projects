About Us

Priyatech Solutions: Premier Turnkey IT Solution Provider

Founded in 1997, Priyatech Solutions is a premier turnkey solution provider based in Pune, India. With a technically proficient and experienced team, we excel in delivering comprehensive and timely IT solutions for Banking, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Educational Institutions, and Government sectors. We offer top-tier hardware installations, both COTS and customized software solutions, extensive training, technical manpower, and facility management services. Our commitment to educating clients and providing the best-suited IT solutions enhances their processes, driven by our core values of quality, integrity, and simplicity.
